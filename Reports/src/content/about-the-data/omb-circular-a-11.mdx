This Office of Management and Budget (OMB) document governs
requirements for preparing, submitting, and executing the federal
budget. It affects USAspending.gov in outlining many key concepts,
definitions, and data elements used within Files A, B and C, and it
also includes congressional budget justification posting requirements.
