This Office of Management and Budget (OMB) memorandum establishes
Intragovernmental Transfer (IGT) reporting requirements, requirements
for reporting financial assistance awards containing personally
identifiable information (PII), and requirements that senior
accountable officials (SAO) provide public assurance on a quarterly
basis that their agency's internal controls support the validity and
reliability of data reported to USAspending.
