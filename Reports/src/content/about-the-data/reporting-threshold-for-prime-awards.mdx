All federal award transactions with obligation value of \$25,000 and
above must be reported to USAspending.gov, as per
<a target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer" href="https://uscode.house.gov/view.xhtml?req=(title:31%20section:6101%20edition:prelim)%20OR%20(granuleid:USC-prelim-title31-section6101)&amp;f=treesort&amp;edition=prelim&amp;num=0&amp;jumpTo=true">31 USC ยง 6101 note</a>.

Additionally, Federal agencies must report all contract and
financial assistance awards that are above a micro-purchase
threshold to the
<a target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer" href="https://www.fpds.gov/">Federal Procurement Data System (FPDS</a>), Financial Assistance Broker Submission (FABS), and USAspending. gov. The
requirement for agencies to report contact awards above a
micro-purchase threshold is in Federal Acquisition Regulation (FAR) 4.606
(<a target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer" href="https://www.ecfr.gov/current/title-48/chapter-1/subchapter-A/part-4/subpart-4.6/section-4.606">48 CFR ยง 4.606</a>),

USAspending.gov is set forth in
<a target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer" href="https://www.ecfr.gov/current/title-2/subtitle-A/chapter-I/part-170/subpart-B/section-170.200">2 CFR ยง 170.200</a>.
